Megalithic structures

Is a prehistoric monument made of large stones. The word Megalithic, comes from the Greek; "mega" meaning, large, and "lithos" meaning, stone. Megalithic structures can be found throughout Europe, Russia, the Americas, Africa and Asia. Even though they are very distant from each other.

There are three types of megaliths

Menhir

It consists of a large block of stone with an elongated shape and vertically nailed to the ground. It is believed that it could have been used to worship the sun or a deceased person of certain prestige in the village, or to mark a territorial extension.

Dolmen

It makes reference to constructions of several vertical stones that support a horizontal one or to other more complex constructions that have a chamber and, in occasions, of a corridor through which one accedes to that main chamber.
