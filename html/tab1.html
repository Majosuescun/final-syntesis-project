Celestial coordinates

Celestial Coordinates are the set of values that, according to a given reference system, give the position of an object on the celestial sphere. There are different celestial Coordinates depending on their origin and plane reference.

Basic concepts

What is latitude?

It is the angle between the parallel of a point and the quator.

Latitude of Medellín

Medellín is located in the geographic center of the Aburrá Valley, on the central mountain range of the Andes, at coordinates 6°13′55″N 75°34′05″W).

What is longitude?

It is the angle between the meridian of a point and the zero meridian (Greenwich). Right ascension and declination coordinates define a coordinate system on the celestial sphere.
